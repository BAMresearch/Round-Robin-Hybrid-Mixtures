Within the framework of the WiPaNo joint research project „NEX-HYS - development of a standardized method
for the determintation of safety characterisics of hybrid mixtures“ the following institutions are experimnentally
involved:
Berlin - Bundesanstalt für Materialforschung und -prüfung
Braunschweig - Physikalisch Technische Bundesanstalt
Magdeburg - Otto-von-Guericke Universität
Hamm - Inburex Consulting
The german institute for standardization - DIN e.V. - accompanies the project on the way to a new standard.
