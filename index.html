Within the framework of the WiPaNo joint research project „NEX-HYS - development of a standardized method
for the determintation of safety characterisics of hybrid mixtures“ the following institutions are experimnentally
involved:
Berlin - Bundesanstalt für Materialforschung und -prüfung
Braunschweig - Physikalisch Technische Bundesanstalt
Magdeburg - Otto-von-Guericke Universität
Hamm - Inburex Consulting
The german institute for standardization - DIN e.V. - accompanies the project on the way to a new standard.

Berlin - Spitzer et al. (2021) - Comparative study on standardized ignition sources used for explosion testing
Braunschweig - Zakel et. al. (2019) - Safety characteristics of hybrid mixtures for explosion protection
Magdeburg - Addai et. al. (2016) - Minimum ignition energy of hybrid mixtures of combustible dusts and gases
Magdeburg - Gabel et. al. (2015) - Lower explosion limit of hybrid mixtures
Karlsruhe - Denkevits and Hoess (2015) - Hybrid H2/Al dust explosions in Siwek sphere
Karlsruhe - Denkevits (2007) - Explosibility of hydrogen–graphite dust hybrid mixtures
Zürich - Georg Michael Schuber (1987) - Zünddurchschlagverhalten von StaubLuft- und hybriden Gemischen durch enge Spalte
Basel - Siwek (1996) - Determination of technical safety indices and factors influencing hazard evaluation of dusts
